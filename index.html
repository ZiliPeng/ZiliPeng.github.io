Short Bio

I am currently a researcher and image processing engineer. I received my Ph.D. degree from the College of Mathematics and Computer Science, Hunan Normal University with honor in Dec. 2018. My current research focus is on deep learning for computer vision and image processing, e.g., semantic segmentation, instance segmentation, object detection, object(s) tracking, and some applications based on 3D model of human body pose, hand pose, and facial expression. For more information, please refer to my CV.
